About Us

We Bring Changes to
Rural Karnataka

  • Our Mission
  • Our Vision
  • Our History

To empower the underserved by providing access to quality education, professional vocational skills, and sustainable livelihood opportunities.

  • Free Quality Education
  • Youth Skill Development
  • Women's Financial Independence
  • Preventive Healthcare Camps

To create a self-reliant society where every child is educated, every youth is skilled, and every woman is an empowered entrepreneur.

  • 30% Dropout Rate Reduction
  • 100% Youth Employment Goals
  • Sustainable Rural Development
  • Global Standard Training

Founded on May 25, 2004, under the Society Registration Act, OM Samsthe started with a vision to serve Bheemarayanagudi and has since impacted 2,500+ lives.

  • Est. May 2004 (Shahapur)
  • NGO Darpan Registered
  • 80G & 12A Certified
  • CSR-1 Form Approved
What we deliver

Empowering Underserved Communities
Together with You

Free Education

We provide high-quality schooling and moral values to children from underprivileged backgrounds to ensure holistic growth.

Skill Training

Our vocational programs equip youth and women with market-relevant skills like computer literacy and tailoring.

Women Empowerment

We uplift women through self-help groups, leadership training, and financial literacy to foster independence.

Health & Nutrition

We organize health camps and hygiene drives to ensure the physical well-being of the communities we serve.
