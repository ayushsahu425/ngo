About Us

Our Mission to Change The World

OM Vidyavardhaka Shikshana Samsthe, established in 2004 and based in Yadgir, Karnataka, is a nongovernmental organization dedicated to fostering education, skill development, and women empowerment. The organization focuses on providing free education to children from underserved communities and empowering women through various capacity-building initiatives. With two decades of service, OM Vidyavardhaka Shikshana Samsthe has become a cornerstone in promoting sustainable development in the region, ensuring equal opportunities for education and skills for all.

From the Director's Desk

Leading with Compassion & Vision

Since our inception in 2004, OM Vidyavardhaka Shikshana Samsthe has been driven by a single mission: to bridge the gap between opportunity and the underserved communities of Karnataka. We believe that education and self-reliance are the strongest pillars of a thriving society.

Our work in Manpower Security Training and Women Entrepreneurship is not just about providing jobs; it's about restoring dignity. With a 30% reduction in rural school dropouts and over 1,200 women empowered, we are witnessing a generational shift. We invite you to be a part of this journey as we continue to nurture talent and protect the future of our youth.
